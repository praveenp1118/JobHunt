Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

1. Introduction

AIJobsHunt is an AI-powered job-search platform operated by Praveen Prakash (“we”, “us”, “our”), based in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the rights you have. We comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the EU/EEA and with India’s applicable data-protection laws (Information Technology Act, 2000).

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data — your name, email, and password (stored only as a bcrypt hash, never in plain text).
  • CV data — your CV content, stored encrypted at rest (AES-256).
  • Job data — the job descriptions you search, save, and track.
  • API keys — your Anthropic API key and optional Apify token, stored AES-256 encrypted and never displayed again after you save them.
  • Usage logs — which AI operations ran, token counts, and estimated costs (retained up to 90 days).
  • Payment data — we store only your Stripe customer ID. Card details are handled entirely by Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1 certified); we never see or store them.
  • Chat messages — support conversations (retained 1 year).

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide job-search, CV-tailoring, and application-tracking services.
  • To score job fit using Anthropic’s Claude AI.
  • To scan job boards on your behalf (via your Apify token).
  • To send job applications via your Gmail account (with your explicit action).

We never use your data to train AI models. We never sell or share your data with third parties for advertising or any other purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on a small number of processors strictly to deliver the service:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — your CV and job descriptions are sent to the Anthropic API for AI processing. Anthropic does not train on data submitted via its API. anthropic.com/legal/privacy
  • Apify — job listings are scraped using your own Apify token. Only public job-board data is processed. apify.com/privacy-policy
  • Stripe — payment processing, PCI DSS Level 1 certified. We store only your Stripe customer ID. stripe.com/privacy
  • Gmail (Google) — accessed via IMAP/SMTP using credentials you provide, to read job-alert emails and send applications on your instruction.

5. Data Retention

  • Active account — data is kept while your account is active.
  • Expired subscription — data is kept for 30 days.
  • Deleted account — all your data is purged within 30 days.
  • Usage logs — up to 90 days.
  • Chat messages — 1 year.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Right to access — request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure — delete your account and all associated data.
  • Right to portability — a JSON export of your data.
  • Right to rectification — edit your data at any time in the app.

To exercise these rights, use Settings → Privacy in the app, or email praveenp.1118@gmail.com.

7. Security

  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data (CV content, API keys).
  • bcrypt password hashing.
  • JWT authentication (7-day expiry; 30 days with “remember me”).
  • API keys are never exposed to the frontend after saving.
  • Our code is open source: github.com/praveenp1118/JobHunt

8. Cookies

We use only an essential session cookie for authentication. We use no advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics (no Google Analytics). See our Cookie Policy for details.

9. Contact

Email praveenp.1118@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 48 hours.

10. Changes

We will notify users by email of any material changes to this policy.
