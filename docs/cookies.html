1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. They let a website remember things between requests — for example, that you are logged in.

2. Cookies we use

We use essential cookies only:

Cookie Purpose Duration Type
jobhunt_session Keeps you logged in (JWT token) 7 days (30 days with “remember me”) Essential — cannot be disabled

That’s it — no other cookies.

3. What we don’t use

  • ✗ No advertising cookies
  • ✗ No tracking pixels
  • ✗ No Google Analytics
  • ✗ No Facebook Pixel
  • ✗ No third-party tracking
  • ✗ No cross-site tracking

4. Your choices

You can clear cookies at any time in your browser settings. Clearing jobhunt_session will log you out of JobHunt.
